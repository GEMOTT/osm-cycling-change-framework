OSM validation method

Aim

Validate OSM cycling infrastructure in Barcelona across years 2019 and 2023.

Design

  • Stratified random sample of census tracts (centrality × density, 3×3).

  • Five tracts per stratum (45 in total).

  • Per tract and year:

    • 3 random points on the OSM cycleway network (if present in tract) → check for false positives for that year.

    • 3 random points on the OSM general road network → check for false negative for that year.

  • Validation source: Google Street View (GSV).
